Ticket Guidelines
How to Submit an Effective Ticket
  • Be clear and specific about the issue you're experiencing
  • Include steps to reproduce the problem
  • Add any error messages you've received
  • Mention when the issue started occurring
  • Attach screenshots or relevant files if applicable
Priority Levels
  • Low Priority

    Minor issues that don't impact your work. Typically addressed within 48-72 hours.

  • Medium Priority

    Issues that cause inconvenience but have workarounds. Typically addressed within 24-48 hours.

  • High Priority

    Issues that significantly impact your work. Typically addressed within 4-24 hours.

  • Critical Priority

    Issues that completely prevent work or affect multiple users. Typically addressed within 1-4 hours.
